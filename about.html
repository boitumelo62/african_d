African Diamond

We As The African Diamond family Prides Ourselves with ensuring that everything that comes from us represents who we are.

We are a brand that values pristine quality, crystal excellence and finessed exclusivity while making certain to keep integrity in check to ensure that we remain rooted to the core of who we are as true legacy creators of divine perfection.

Our Story

After our quest throughout Africa for the perfect location to source our grapes and begin the manufacturing process, we settled on South Africa.

Given the country's overwhelming variety of grapes and wine we were faced with the arduous -albeit enjoyable- task of finding the perfect grapes for each African Diamond wine. This is because our dream has been to introduce to you a wine with pre-eminence and shared on special occasions with those dearest to you. Our wines have far-reaching impact to interconnect and cultivate connections not only for those within Africa, but further into the rest of the world.

Vision

Introducing a carefully-crafted, exclusive wine with class. Presenting delectable quality in a bottle so delightful it unlocks important notes, memories and represents momentous milestones with every sip.

Appreciate the pure velvety tickle on the palate infused with rich leathery notes and aromas. A wine which speaks to those who have ambition and passion to succeed. Enjoy The Billionaire's Choice.
